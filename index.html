Why Use River Pebbles for Succulents?

River pebbles provide a natural look and help prevent soil erosion and compaction. Their excellent drainage properties reduce the risk of root rot and fungal diseases, making them ideal for desert plants like succulents and cacti.

Top-dressing with pebbles also improves moisture retention in the root zone while keeping the surface dry, which helps deter pests like fungus gnats.
